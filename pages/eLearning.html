About the platform

Forethought’s eLearning platform provides structured, accessible learning modules that support participants, families, support workers, and professionals to better understand recovery‑oriented practice, behaviour support principles, and system navigation.

Content is developed using plain‑language principles and trauma‑aware design, with a focus on practical application, consent‑led practice, and real‑world scenarios.

  • Clear, structured modules
  • Accessible layouts and readable content
  • Designed for varied literacy levels and learning styles
  • Aligned with ethical and governance‑focused practice

Who it’s for

  • NDIS participants
  • Families and carers
  • Support coordinators and practitioners
  • New staff and peer workers
  • Organisations seeking consistent practice guidance

Access may vary depending on service engagement or organisational arrangements.

Module topics

Foundations of Recovery‑Oriented Practice

Understanding strengths‑based approaches, participant choice, and dignity in recovery‑focused support.

Behaviour Support Principles

Plain‑language explanations of behaviour support, functional understanding, and positive approaches.

Consent & Ethical Practice

Clear guidance on consent, information sharing, and documentation‑led decision making.

NDIS System Navigation

Understanding roles, plan reviews, evidence, and navigating systems with confidence.

Access & availability

The eLearning platform may be accessed:

  • As part of service engagement
  • For staff induction and ongoing learning
  • Through organisation‑based access arrangements

Modules may be completed at the learner’s own pace, with progress saved between sessions.
