Clear, detail-focused support for ethics and governance submissions — designed to reduce delays, strengthen documentation, and align processes with best-practice requirements.

  • Governance documentation
  • Participant information and consent forms
  • Consumer consultation
  • GCP-aligned document review
  • Support for multi-site and international studies

Who it’s for

Researchers, research teams, and organisations preparing ethics submissions, governance packs, or participant-facing documents and consent processes.
