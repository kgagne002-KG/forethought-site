Outcomes & Testimonials

Anonymised vignettes showing measurable progress aligned to individual goals (no identifying details).

Outcomes vary by person and circumstances. We focus on progress markers, documentation and participant choice.

Vignettes

Recovery Coaching — Community Participation

Situation: Participant sought support to increase community engagement after plan changes reduced supports.

Approach: Strengths mapping, coaching block, warm referrals to local groups.

Outcome: Increased confidence and regular participation aligned to personal goals within 12 weeks.

Behaviour Support — Practical Implementation

Situation: Behaviour of concern impacted participation and consistency.

Approach: Functional assessment, co‑designed PBS plan, implementation coaching and staff training.

Outcome: Clearer approaches and reduced need for restrictive responses over time.

Plan Review Support — Evidence & Advocacy

Situation: Plan review risk with limited evidence and under‑funding concerns.

Approach: Evidence summary, outcome markers, advocacy brief and preparation.

Outcome: Stronger plan alignment to goals and clearer pathways to required supports.
